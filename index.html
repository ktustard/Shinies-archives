About Sheena

The Main Dancer of BINI

Sheena Catacutan is known for her exceptional dancing, energetic personality, infectious smile, and dedication as the Main Dancer of BINI. Throughout her journey, she has inspired countless fans through hard work, perseverance, and her genuine love for performing.

Sheena's Journey

Featured

Career Eras

PBB Otso (2018)

Sheena's first television appearance where the public discovered her talent, determination, and vibrant personality.

Star Hunt Academy (2019–2020)

Underwent intensive idol training before becoming one of the eight official members of BINI.

Da Coconut Nut (2020)

BINI's pre-debut era featuring their remake of the iconic Filipino classic that introduced the group to the public.

Born To Win (2021)

The official debut of BINI, marking the beginning of Sheena's professional career as an idol.

Feel Good (2022)

The era that showcased BINI's signature bright sound through songs like I Feel Good and Lagi.

Pantropiko / Talaarawan (2023–2024)

The breakthrough era featuring Karera, Pantropiko, Salamin, Salamin, and Cherry On Top, introducing BINI to millions worldwide.

BINIverse World Tour (2024–2025)

BINI's first major international concert tour that expanded their global fanbase and laid the foundation for worldwide recognition.

Coachella Era (2026)

BINI became the first Filipino act to perform at Coachella, bringing P-pop to one of the world's biggest music festivals.

Signals Era (2026–Present)

The global era highlighted by the Signals album, Summer Sonic Japan, worldwide promotions, and continued international expansion.
