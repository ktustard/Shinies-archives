Journey

Sheena's Journey

2018

Pinoy Big Brother: Otso

Sheena Catacutan first gained public attention as a contestant on Pinoy Big Brother: Otso. Although her journey inside the house was brief, it became the first major step toward her dream of becoming an artist.

2019

Star Hunt Academy

After PBB, Sheena was accepted into the Star Hunt Academy (SHA), where she underwent intensive training in dancing, singing, stage performance, and teamwork to prepare for her future debut.

2020

Official Introduction as BINI Member

Sheena graduated as one of the eight final SHA trainees and was officially introduced as the eighth member of BINI.

In November, BINI released its pre-debut single, Da Coconut Nut, a modern electropop remake of the Ryan Cayabyab classic.

2021

Official Debut — Born to Win Era

On June 4, 2021, BINI officially debuted with Born to Win. Later that year, the group released its first full album featuring songs such as Kapit Lang, marking the beginning of their professional career.

2022

Feel Good Era

BINI released their second studio album, Feel Good, introducing the bright, youthful bubblegum pop sound that would later become one of the group's signatures. Hit songs included I Feel Good and Lagi.

2023

Karera & Pantropiko

BINI released Karera and Pantropiko. Pantropiko quickly became one of the biggest songs in the Philippines, dominating streaming platforms and social media while introducing BINI to an even wider audience.

2024

Talaarawan Era

On March 8, BINI released their first EP, Talaarawan, featuring the chart-topping single Salamin, Salamin.

In September, the group released Cherry on Top, which later received a remix featuring Indonesian superstar Agnez Mo.

2025

FLAMES Era

BINI surpassed 1 Billion Spotify streams, released the single Blink Twice, and unveiled their third studio album, FLAMES, continuing their rise as one of Asia's leading P-pop groups.

2026

Coachella & Summer Sonic

On April 10, BINI made history as the first Filipino act to perform at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival.

In August, the group brought the Signals World Tour to Japan by performing at the legendary Summer Sonic Festival in Osaka and Tokyo, further establishing BINI on the global stage.
