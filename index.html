About Sheena

The Main Dancer of BINI

Sheena Catacutan is known for her exceptional dancing, powerful stage presence, stable vocals, energetic personality, and infectious smile as the Main Dancer of BINI. Beyond her captivating performances, she has inspired countless fans through her unwavering dedication, hard work, perseverance, and genuine love for music and performing, proving that passion and determination can leave a lasting impact on everyone who watches her.
